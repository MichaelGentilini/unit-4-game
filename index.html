Coin Collector Game

The user will be provided with a random number at the beginning of the game

There are four ancient coins below. By clicking on a coin, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

You lose if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.

Good Luck!
